Introducing Unity College

Unity College is an unaided English medium, Co-educational College established by a registered philanthropic society,the Tauheedul Muslimeen Trust Foundation Society, Lucknow. Today it has about 2500 students, above 100 staff members and classes from Lower Nursery to class XII. Unity College is affiliated with the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations, New Delhi..

Unity College began in 1984, in a rented building with only seventeen students. It has since developed into a much larger campus that educates about 2100 students in grades lower nursery through XII on terrain that spreads across almost six acres, sustaining more than 64,000 square feet of classsroom and administrative buildings.

These modern facilities accommodate classrooms, fully equipped Science and Computer labs, an expanding library, an assembly hall that seats 400 students, a cafeteria, infirmary and a playground that is maintained for sports and other activities. The school and its curriculum are affiliated with the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations (CISCE) in New Delhi.

At present, eighty well-qualified teachers present instruction in English in a manner that supports the motto of the college: Excellence in Education. The students come from underprivileged families and are educated in a broad spectrum of subjects - in the higher grades of X and XII, they have the opportunity to choose between science and commerce majors.
